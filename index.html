Welcome to the Solar System

Explore the planets and learn fun facts about each one!

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and has extreme temperature changes!

Venus

Venus has a day longer than its year!

Earth

Earth is the only planet known to support life.

Mars

Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons!

Jupiter

Jupiter has a storm called the Great Red Spot that has lasted for hundreds of years!

Saturn

Saturn is known for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock!

Uranus

Uranus is tilted at an extreme angle of 98 degrees!

Neptune

Neptune is the windiest planet in the solar system!
